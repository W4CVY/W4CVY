CHATTAHOOCHEE VALLEY AMATEUR RADIO NET

Every Wednesday evening at 8:00 Eastern time
on the club repeater 146.880 MHz with a (-) offset with a PL tone of 123

All licensed Hams are encouraged to check in.

NET SCRIPT

CHATTAHOOCHEE VALLEY NET

CQ CQ CQ - This is the Chattahoochee Valley Net. This is (your name), (your call), and my QTH is (your QTH). I am the net control station for this session.

Is there any emergency or priority traffic?

This net is open to all hams with privileges on this frequency. We meet every Wednesday at 8:30 PM ET on the 146.88 Columbus Amateur Radio Club repeater. This repeater has a minus offset and 123 PL tone. The alternate repeater frequency is 146.745, with a minus offset and 123.0 tone. In the event that both repeaters are down we will meet on 146.51 simplex. The purpose of the net is to train members to be proficient during emergencies, disseminate important information, and to encourage fellowship. This is a directed net, so please direct all traffic to the net control station. This is not a traffic handling net, however we will be glad to pass any traffic listed. This is (your call) and the Chattahoochee Valley Net.

Are there any bulletins or announcements?

Check-ins

Please announce your call sign phonetically, give your name and your location. If any operator wishes to close, please announce that you will “secure your station”.

At this time I will take check-ins from any mobile stations. Mobile stations, please call.

(Take comments from mobile stations before continuing.)

At this time I will take check-ins from fixed stations.

(Allow all fixed stations to check in before going back for comments.)

1. Stations with suffixes beginning ALPHA - INDIA

2. Stations with suffixes beginning JULIETT - ROMEO

3. Stations with suffixes beginning SIERRA - ZULU

4. Any station ALPHA – ZULU


 (Return to top of the roll and take comments from all fixed stations)

This is (your call) and the Chattahoochee Valley Net. Are there any additional stations wishing to check in, please call now.

(Take comments from the late check-ins)

Technical Corner:

This portion of the net is for any discussion of technical items related to ham radio. Does anyone have anything to share on the Technical Corner?

Swap Net:

You may swap, sell or give away any item that can be used with amateur radio. You may give a price over the air, but please do not negotiate a price on the air. Do we have any listings for the Chattahoochee Valley Swap Net?

Late Check Ins:

This is (your call) and the Chattahoochee Valley Net. Do we have any late check-ins?

(Take comments from latest check-ins.)

Close:
