Emergency Operations

The Columbus Amateur Radio Club is always on alert for and during emergency conditions.  We are especially alert during severe weather situations which occur often in this area of the country.

The club works closely with local emergency agencies, such as the Emergency Management Office, Police, Red Cross Chapters and others in the area.  These are agencies which can use assistance with communications during situations where commerical power and communications may not be available for some time.
