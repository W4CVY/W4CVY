CHATTAHOOCHEE VALLEY AMATEUR RADIO NET

Every Wednesday evening at 8:00 Eastern time
on the club repeater 146.880 MHz with a (-) offset with a PL tone of 123

All licensed Hams are encouraged to check in.

(Pause)

This net is open to all hams with privileges on this frequency.
We meet every Wednesday at 8:00 PM ET on the 146.88 Columbus Amateur Radio Club repeater.
This repeater has a minus offset and 123 PL tone.

The alternate repeater frequency is 146.745, also with a minus offset and 123 tone.
In the event that both repeaters are down we will meet on 146.55 simplex.

The purpose of the net is to train members to be proficient during emergencies, disseminate important information, and to encourage fellowship.
This is a directed net, so please direct all traffic to the net control station.
This is not a traffic handling net, however we will be glad to pass any traffic listed.
This is (your call) with the Chattahoochee Valley Net.

Are there any bulletins or announcements? (Pause)

At this time I will take check-ins from any mobile stations.
Mobile stations, please call.
