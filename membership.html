The Columbus Amateur Radio Club asks its members to pay dues in the amount of $24 per year.
 These dues are used to pay for:

  - Upkeep of club equipment,
  - Power Bills to run the club's repeaters,
  - Insurance Premiums covering club equipment and activities,
  - Expenses on Field Day and other club activities, and occasionally
  - Make purchases of equipment or Supplies on the club's behalf.
